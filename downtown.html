Downtown

Downtown Eau Claire is located right along the confluence of the Chippewa and Eau Claire Rivers. This area is full of art, hip bars and restaurants, and live music. Home to over 400 business downtown Eau Claire has alot to offer. Here we highlight a few of our favorite attractions.

The Pablo Center at the Confluence

The center offers the region a facility capable of providing transformative training, learning and creative opportunities. Inside, guests, local partners, and artists experience a 1,200+ seat theatre and an approximately 400-seat flexible theatre; three rehearsal, dance and community rooms; visual arts galleries; labs for sound and lighting, set and exhibit design, recording arts, multimedia production and costume design; and spaces equipped to support vocational training initiatives.

The Local Store

The Local Store is chocked full of gift-worthy goods. Whether your seeking an offering of art, apparel, books, music, kids gear, glassware, decor, games, or foodstuffs for friends, family, or yourself, you'll surely find something deserving of your gift-giving generosity. Since many of their 1,000 items - products either created by local craftspeople or products simply evoking a Wisconsin way of life - are homemade designs and we offer exclusive in-house brands, the goodies provided by The Local Store are both distinctive and memorable - two ideal qualities of a great gift!

Website

Volume One Gallery

The Volume One Gallery is a part of The Local Store, The vintage space features high ceilings, old wood floors, exposed brick walls and full A/V capability including an in-house sound system, adjustable room and stage lighting, and a projector/screen combo. Between the Gallery and lobby seating area, more than 1,000 square feet are available for both public and private events. The Gallery is open with The Local Store every day, and features a rotating art exhibit from local and regional artists.

L.E. Phillips Memorial Public Library

L.E. Phillips Memorial Public Library is the largest library in west central Wisconsin and provides its customers with fiction and non-fiction books; magazines and newspapers; music CDs; DVDs; books-on-CD; downloadable e-books, audiobooks and music; educational computer software; programming for kids, teens and adults; Internet access and free Wi-Fi; word-processing computers and online information databases and resources; meeting rooms; art exhibits and displays; answers in person, by phone or e-mail; information about community agencies & services; local history and genealogy resources; delivery of materials to the homebound; iPads for checkout; volunteer opportunities; and more.

Downtown Cinema

Experience the charm of Micon Cinemas Downtown Budget Theater’s classic movie theater vibe with modern amenities. From reserved seating, to a full-scale food and beverage menu, enjoy new movie releases on a dime (Adult $5, Youth $4) with the luxury of contemporary movie theaters. From specialty pizzas, appetizers, desserts, alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages, classic movie theater concessions, as well as vegan options, there’s something for every palate!

200 Main Art and Wine

200 MAIN ART & WINE is a vibrant, engaging, FUN fine art gallery located on the corner of Main and Graham Ave., in Downtown Eau Claire. Voted ‘BEST ART GALLERY’ for 2018, they have expanded and are now the first art gallery to host a wine dispenser, with options for 8 different kinds of wonderful wines to enjoy as you view fine art and relax with friends (beer available as well). Over 30 local and regional artists are represented in this comfortable, creative environment! This vibrant gallery also sponsors creative events, such as author readings, live music, art demonstrations and group discussions.

B-Framed Galleries

B~Framed Galleries two story art gallery has been located in historic Downtown Eau Claire since 1976. When you walk into the gallery you will be surrounded with incredible art that you will not see anywhere else. They feature International and nationally known artists as well as exclusive Wisconsin art and exclusive regional photography as well as three dimensional fine art glass, ceramics and wood. Their expansive two gallery inventory and exclusive resources assure that they can find the perfect piece of art for your home or office.
