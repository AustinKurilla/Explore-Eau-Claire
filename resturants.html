Dooley's Pub

Dooley's Pub has been the place to go for a traditional Irish pub experience since doors opened in 2005. Whether you're looking for a hearty Irish stew made with Guinness gravy, or cold glass of Finnegan's Irish Amber, you're sure to find what you're looking for here.

Mogie's Pub

Mogie's Pub is Home of Eau Claire's greatest burgers. They offer fun, casual atmosphere, 17 tap beers, full bar, and televised sporting events. Located on historic Water Street. Dine-in or carry out.

ClaireWaters is bringing back the supper club with a twist and a high level of excellence to the westside of Eau Claire. You'll find a wide array of food from cheese curds to ahi tuna wontons, chicken satay and sliders to Norwegian smoked salmon sandwiches. Also a great place to watch the game.

Fuji Sushi and Steak House

Fuji Sushi & Steakhouse is a wonderful full service steakhouse and sushi bar that combines fresh sushi with authentic Japanese hibachi entrees. All prepared in front of you as you sit at the grill and watch your dish being made from start to finish.
